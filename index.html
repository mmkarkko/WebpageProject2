    About

    Ever since I was little, I have gravitated towards computers and technology. After trials and errors, here I am with a Bachelor of Science degree in Economics and Business Administration. I have discovered a deep passion for front-end development, an area where I aim to specialize and contribute meaningfully.

    My academic journey, coupled with hands-on experience, has equipped me with a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and a growing interest in JavaScript, making these my strongest skills. In addition, I have a good foundation in programming languages, such as Java and C#. I am also familiar with Python and SQL as well, which further complement my set of skills and provide me with understanding of both front- and back-end development.

    Throughout my life, I have created several websites that have been published online. Although these sites are no longer available, they represent a significant part of my ongoing interest in web development and have continuously enhanced my skills. Working on these projects, I have found that I excel in creating responsive web pages and interactive user interfaces, combining my technical skills with my eye for design. My experience in graphic design further complements my web development abilities, allowing me to deliver visually appealing and user-friendly web experiences.

    In addition to my web development skills, I have a strong understanding of cybersecurity principles, gained through my academic studies. This knowledge enables me to implement best practices for web security, ensuring that the applications I develop are secure and resilient against common threats.

    Tackling challenges with a problem-solving mindset, I excel in communication both in Finnish and English. I value inclusivity in the workplace and thrive in environments that offer opportunities for continuous self-improvement. Excited to contribute my skills to collaborative teams and projects, aiming to deliver significant results in the field of web development. I thrive on opportunities to apply my passion and diverse skill set to drive impactful outcomes.
